About XRBitcoinCash (XRBC)

XRBitcoinCash is a community-driven token on the XRP Ledger, launched Jan 5, 2022. With a fixed 21 million supply and a blackholed issuer, XRBC is built for predictable tokenomics, security, and long-term transparency.

FAQ

Why is a blackholed issuer important?

It prevents further token issuance or parameter changes, supporting immutability and predictable supply.

Do I need a trust line to hold XRBC?

Yes. Add the XRBC trust line in your XRPL wallet once, then you can receive and trade XRBC.

Where can I trade XRBC?

Use XRPL DEX frontends such as XPMarket and Sologenic. See the Trade page for links.
