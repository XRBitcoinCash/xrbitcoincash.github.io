About XRBitcoinCash

Founded January 5th, 2022, XRBitcoinCash (XRBC) is a token built on the XRP Ledger to enable fast, low-cost, and secure transactions worldwide.

Total Supply: 21 million XRBC

XRBC is blackholed, meaning no additional tokens can be issued.
