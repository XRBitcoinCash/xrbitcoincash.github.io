About XRBitcoinCash (XRBC)

XRBitcoinCash is a community-driven token on the XRP Ledger with a fixed supply and blackholed issuer. Built for resilience, transparency, and accessible on-ledger utility.

Mission

Empower traders and builders on the XRPL with a transparent asset and an open ecosystem.

  • Predictable supply and immutability
  • Low fees and fast finality on XRPL
  • Community collaboration and tools
