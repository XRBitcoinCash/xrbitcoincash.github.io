About XRBitcoinCash

Origin & Purpose

XRBitcoinCash (XRBC) was launched in 2022, in the midst of the SEC vs. Ripple proceedings, as both a gesture of solidarity with the XRPL ecosystem and a living demonstration that tokens on XRPL can be built for clarity, low fees, and provable accountability. The timing underscores the project’s belief that decentralized tokens on XRPL are not securities, but tools for value exchange aligned with open standards.

Mission

Deliver a simple, transparent asset on XRPL with low fees, fast settlement, and clear, auditable flows. Every design decision and integration — from frontend UI to ledger interactions — is governed by that mission.

Principles

  • Clarity over complexity — public token details, readable docs, minimal hidden behavior.
  • Non-custodial by default — users always retain control of their private keys; trades happen on-ledger.
  • Consistent branding & metadata — predictable identifiers and standards across wallets, explorers, and tooling.

How XRBC Works

XRBC uses XRPL’s built-in trustlines and decentralized exchange (DEX) logic. Users first open a trustline to XRBC, then place orders or interact with AMM pools. Trades settle on-ledger, with no centralized custody or middleman. The DEX page and wallet UI in your browser / wallet connect flows simply submit signed transactions to the XRPL network.
