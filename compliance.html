[ Global Compliance Matrix (high-level) ]
Jurisdiction Framework / Focus What typically triggers licensing XRBC posture (non-custodial issuer)
Global (FATF) Travel Rule (Rec.16) & VASP guidance Intermediating transfers (custody, exchange as a business) XRBC operates without custody or client funds. Pricing is on-ledger; no KYC role. Travel Rule obligations fall on custodial VASPs used by participants.
EU (MiCA) CASP licensing; whitepapers; market abuse; stablecoins first Operating as a CASP (custody, exchange, order execution, etc.) XRBC runs public docs + manifests; no customer custody, no order execution service. Exchanges/wallets listing XRBC must comply as CASPs where applicable.
UK (FCA) Financial promotions regime; MLR registration for firms Communicating/approving crypto promotions; operating a business XRBC uses factual, non-promotional language and risk warnings; no UK consumer call-to-action to invest; no UK regulated services provided.
USA (FinCEN / OFAC) MSB if money transmission; sanctions compliance Exchanging/custody as a business; moving value for others No custody, no exchange business, no customer funds. We avoid serving sanctioned persons or regions; DEX usage depends on user wallets/platforms.
Singapore (MAS) Payment Services Act (DPT services) DPT service provider activities (e.g., exchange, transfer for others) XRBC is an issued token on XRPL; no DPT services are offered by the project. Exchanges/wallets must hold MAS approvals where required.
Japan (FSA) Payment Services Act; Crypto-asset Exchange registration Operating a crypto-asset exchange / custody No exchange operated; no custody; on-ledger token only. Listings handled by JFSA-regulated exchanges where applicable.
Korea (FSC) Virtual Asset User Protection Act Operating virtual-asset services (custody/exchange) XRBC does not provide VASP services. Local exchanges/wallets bear compliance duties (listing, user protection, disclosures).
Hong Kong (SFC) VATP licensing; retail access guardrails Operating a VATP; marketing to HK public No VATP operated. Public docs include risk warnings; any HK access is via SFC-licensed VATPs that choose to list XRBC.
UAE (VARA / ADGM) VASPs licensing rulebooks Operating exchange, brokerage, custody No VASP operations by XRBC. If a UAE VASP lists XRBC, their licensing applies. Project publishes machine-readable facts only.
Canada (CSA / FINTRAC) CTP undertakings; MSB / Travel Rule Operating a CTP or MSB activities No CTP/MSB role. Canadian platforms listing XRBC must comply with CSA/FINTRAC requirements.
Mainland China Restrictions/bans on crypto trading Operating crypto business / marketing domestically XRBC does not operate services or solicit users in mainland China.

This matrix is high-level (not exhaustive). Always seek jurisdiction-specific legal advice.
