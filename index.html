Get started: Set your trust line

A trust line enables your wallet to hold XRBC on the XRP Ledger.

  1. Choose a compatible wallet — Use Xaman/XUMM or another XRPL wallet that supports trust lines and DEX trading.
  2. Add the XRBC trust line — Follow your wallet’s prompts to add XRBC with the correct currency code and issuer, then sign the transaction.
  3. Trade on DEX — Once your trust line is active, visit a DEX to place orders or swap for XRBC.

FAQ

What is a trust line on the XRPL?

A trust line is a ledger object that allows your XRPL account to hold a specific issued asset like XRBC.

Is the XRBC issuer blackholed?

Yes. Blackholing disables the issuer’s ability to mint or freeze, reinforcing supply immutability.

Where can I trade XRBC?

Community-favorite venues include XPMarket and Sologenic after your trust line is set.
