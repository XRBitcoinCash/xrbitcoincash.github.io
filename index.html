FAQ

What is a trust line on the XRPL?

A trust line allows your wallet to hold and transact a specific issued asset like XRBC. You set it once, then you can receive, hold, and trade that asset.

Is the XRBC issuer blackholed?

Yes. A blackholed issuer means the issuer account is permanently disabled from issuing more tokens or changing settings, supporting immutability.

Where can I trade XRBC?

Use decentralized exchanges that support XRPL assets, such as XPMarket and Sologenic. See Trade for links and tips.
