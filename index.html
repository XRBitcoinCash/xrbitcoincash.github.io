Get started: Set your trust line

A trust line enables your wallet to hold XRBC on the XRPL.

  1. Choose a compatible wallet — Use Xaman/XUMM or another XRPL wallet that supports trust lines and DEX trading.
  2. Add the XRBC trust line — Enter the XRBC currency code and issuer, then sign the transaction.
  3. Trade on DEX — With the trust line active, place orders or swap for XRBC.
