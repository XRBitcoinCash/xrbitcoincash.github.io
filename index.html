XRbitcoincash (XRBC) is a unique digital asset on the XRP Ledger, listed on XPMarket. Our mission is to foster transparent partnerships, empower collectors with reliable information, and promote a global free trade economy. Collaboration is at our core — we work alongside XRPL projects that share our vision of a borderless economy, bridging the gap between banked and unbanked communities through Ripple Labs technology.

The XRBC token is identified by its ticker 'XRBC' and offers the same robust features as other XRPL assets within the decentralized ecosystem. Collectors can access XRBC via XPMarket’s DEX after establishing a TrustLine. We encourage all participants to understand the TrustLine process before engaging in trades.

As regulatory clarity develops, we remain open to refining our approach, ensuring XRBC remains compliant, secure, and valuable for our community.
