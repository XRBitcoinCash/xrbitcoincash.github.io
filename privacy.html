Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 25, 2025

1. Introduction

XRBitcoinCash.com is non-custodial and does not collect on-ledger personal data. The XRP Ledger (XRPL) is a transparent, public blockchain. Every transaction, memo, and balance is visible to anyone using an explorer or node.

2. Public Nature of the XRP Ledger

On the XRPL, account addresses, balances, trust lines, orders, and memos are permanently recorded and publicly accessible. The protocol provides no inherent privacy layer—any on-chain activity can be queried, indexed, and analyzed.

3. Data We Collect on This Site

Off-ledger, we may collect minimal technical data to maintain and secure our website:

  • Server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps)
  • Cookies for navigation, security, and basic analytics
  • Contact form submissions (email addresses and message content)

We do not collect KYC information, wallet addresses, or any personal identifiers unless voluntarily submitted via contact forms.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use Cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enable core site functionality
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Analyze basic usage trends

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent parts of the site from working properly.

5. Third-Party Services & Links

We link to and integrate with official XRPL tools, Xumm, Xaman, and other services solely for user convenience. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review each service’s privacy policy before use.

6. User Responsibility for Anonymity

To remain private on the XRPL, do not include personal information in wallet names, memos, or trust lines. Avoid KYC attestations linked to your address. Anonymity is entirely controlled by the wallet holder.

7. Legal Bases & Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have rights to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold (e.g., contact form submissions). To exercise these rights, email support@xrbitcoincash.com. We will respond within applicable legal timeframes.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect site data, including encrypted communications (HTTPS) and restricted server access. However, no system is entirely risk-free—use discretion when sharing sensitive information.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us to have it removed.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in legal requirements or site operations. The “Last updated” date will indicate when changes were made. Continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of any revisions.

11. Contact Information

For any questions or requests regarding this policy, email support@xrbitcoincash.com.
