Security Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22 • Applies to XRBitcoinCash (XRBC) websites, docs, and public code.

Our commitment

We value the security of the XRBitcoinCash ecosystem and welcome responsible disclosures. If you believe you’ve found a vulnerability, please report it so we can address it quickly and transparently.

How to report

  • Primary contact: security@xrbitcoincash.com
  • Alternate contact: xrbitcoincash@gmail.com
  • PGP/Encryption: https://xrbitcoincash.com/pgp-key.txt
  • Security.txt: /.well-known/security.txt

Include a clear description, affected URLs or components, steps to reproduce (PoC), expected vs. actual behavior, and impact assessment.

Scope

  • In scope: xrbitcoincash.com website and subpages, public repos under the XRBitcoinCash org, and XRBC configuration/metadata files (e.g., /.well-known/security.txt).
  • Out of scope: Third‑party wallets, exchanges, or services not operated by XRBitcoinCash; physical attacks; social engineering.

Testing guidelines

  • Allowed: Non‑destructive testing, read‑only research, and test accounts you control.
  • Do not: Exfiltrate data, modify/destroy content, impact availability (DoS/DDoS), spam/phishing, or access accounts without permission.
  • Privacy first: If you encounter personal/sensitive data, stop and report immediately without copying or sharing it.

Coordinated disclosure & timelines

  • Acknowledgement: We aim to acknowledge within 72 hours.
  • Triage: Target triage within 7 business days.
  • Remediation: Timelines vary by severity; critical issues prioritized. We appreciate a standard 90‑day disclosure window.

Safe harbor

We will not pursue legal action against researchers who act in good faith, follow this policy, avoid privacy violations and service disruption, and give us a reasonable opportunity to remediate before public disclosure.

This safe harbor applies to research conducted under this policy and does not cover unlawful actions or out‑of‑scope testing.

Recognition & rewards

We maintain a public acknowledgements section for high‑impact reports. Monetary bounties are not guaranteed but may be awarded at our discretion based on severity, novelty, and report quality.
