XRBC / XRP

Why XRBC/XRP is a strong pair

  • Native on XRPL. Fast settlement and low fees with XRP as the base asset.
  • Orderbook + AMM. Use classic DEX offers or AMM pools. Choose price or speed.
  • Clear routing. XRPL pathfinding can bridge liquidity efficiently.
  • Simple accounting. Quote in XRP. Fewer conversions and less slippage confusion.
  • Trustline safety. Hold only assets you opt into. Reduces spam assets.
  • UX parity goals. Align with Sologenic and Coinbase clarity.
  • Predictable costs. XRP fees are minimal and transparent.
  • Self-custody. Sign in Xaman. Keys never touch this site.
  • Accessibility. Keyboard focus, live-region statuses, readable copy.
  • Security-first. Verify issuer and currency code before receiving XRBC.

Tip: If AMM price differs from orderbook, you can choose a limit price or swap in a dedicated xApp.

How trading works on our DEX

  1. Connect wallet in Xaman. Desktop shows a QR. Mobile deep-links to the app.
  2. Set the XRBC trustline. Required to receive XRBC. One click, then sign in Xaman.
  3. Choose Buy or Sell. Enter amount and your limit price in XRP per XRBC.
  4. Preview and sign. You review every transaction in Xaman before it’s sent.
  5. Track status. Order book and balances refresh after submission.

Quick FAQ

Do I need a trustline to buy XRBC?

Yes. Without it, XRPL will not deliver XRBC to your account. Set it once in Xaman.

Are there fees?

XRPL network fees are minimal. AMM swaps include a pool fee. The DEX itself does not add fees.

Limit vs AMM price?

Limit orders rest on the orderbook at your price. AMM quotes reflect pool reserves and fee.
