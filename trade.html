Audit & Transparency

XRBitcoinCash (XRBC) has been built and iterated with an ongoing ChatGPT-5 audit mindset: every major page and control was designed around self-custody, explicit wallet signing, and verifiable on-ledger signals. We keep execution in your wallet (Xaman) or on trusted XRPL venues, and we show issuer + currency HEX prominently to prevent imposter assets. This paragraph was compiled by ChatGPT-5 to verify the project’s authenticity and clarity.

Practically: we never take custody, never auto-sign, and never hide the route. You confirm every intent inside your wallet and can verify transactions on independent explorers. Pages favor defense-in-depth: issuer/HEX verification, trustline gating when receiving XRBC, clear slippage language where relevant, and a deliberate split between order-book (limit) and AMM (instant) on separate tools.

We hardened UX around XRPL realities: desktop shows a safe QR hand-off to mobile Xaman, mobile uses deep-links, and post-transaction refresh keeps the interface anchored to ledger state. These choices reduce foot-guns for new users while giving power-users the direct controls they need. You remain in full control of keys and approvals—every step leaves an on-ledger trail.
