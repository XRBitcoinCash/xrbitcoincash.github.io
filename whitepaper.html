1) Introduction

XRBitcoinCash (XRBC) is a fixed‑supply issued token on the XRP Ledger (XRPL) designed to make fast, low‑fee transfers accessible to everyone. The project is an ongoing build: the token was minted prior to the public launch of ChatGPT, but the documentation, security posture, UI copy, and audit guidance have been co‑developed and continuously improved with ChatGPT assistance.

Our north star is transparent, AI‑readable safety: clear on‑ledger identities, machine‑parsable manifests, and live health/price endpoints that third‑party agents (including AI) can verify independently.

3) Security & AI‑Audited Transparency

XRBC maintains a Security Center with health checks, order‑book visibility, and AI‑readable metadata. ChatGPT contributes to documentation, risk language, and review checklists. All reviews are advisory and do not replace independent audits.

  • No API keys / wallet secrets in the repository; keys are rotated regularly.
  • Desktop safety: wallet actions use a QR‑only flow; no direct signing in browser.
  • Health & price pages expose machine‑readable JSON for automated checks.

5) Trading & Liquidity

XRBC trades on the XRPL built‑in DEX. Liquidity is provided and maintained on XRPL AMM pools (Sologenic UI), with a low target fee to reduce arbitrage impact. Users should understand trustlines, order books, slippage, and custody responsibilities before trading.

  • Set trustline in Xaman (Xumm), then trade via XRPL DEX UI.
  • Order‑book prices are fetched live (no static prices).
  • Liquidity pools are funded by the ecosystem founder and maintained on Sologenic AMM.

6) Escrow Roadmap (Issued Tokens)

As XRPL evolves, the project plans to leverage escrow for issued tokens to provide time‑locked, on‑ledger schedules that improve predictability for market participants. The objective is to mirror the transparency ethos of Bitcoin’s issuance while retaining XRPL’s speed and low fees.

  • Time‑locked escrows for treasury allocations.
  • Public schedules with on‑ledger verification.
  • Governance‑aware fee settings for AMM pools.

Implementation details will track XRPL amendments and best practices. All schedules will be disclosed on‑site once finalized.
