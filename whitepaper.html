XRBC Whitepaper

This document outlines the mission, vision, and operational framework of the XRBitcoinCash token, built on the XRP Ledger. It is designed for transparency and accessibility.

1. Introduction — The XRBC token is a unique digital asset leveraging the speed, efficiency, and low transaction costs of the XRPL. Our team is committed to community engagement and forming partnerships that foster a free, transparent global market.

2. Collaboration — We seek alliances with other XRPL projects to establish a borderless economic ecosystem. By integrating Trustlines and decentralized trading via DEX platforms, we aim to bridge the gap between banked and unbanked communities worldwide.

3. Access — Collectors can acquire XRBC via the XPMarket DEX and AMM pools after setting the appropriate Trustline. Details and requirements are clearly outlined on our official portals to ensure informed participation.

4. Compliance & Growth — As regulations evolve, XRBitcoinCash remains adaptable, ensuring compliance while driving innovation. Our goal is to remain a leader within the XRPL ecosystem by prioritizing user trust and global accessibility.

5. Amendment: Future Escrow Functionality for Issued Tokens — As the XRP Ledger evolves, amendments are under development to enable escrow functionality for issued tokens, not only for XRP. Once this feature is activated, XRBitcoinCash (XRBC) will leverage escrow in a way that mirrors the release mechanics of Bitcoin itself.

This means XRBC holders will benefit from:

  • Time-locked escrows that simulate Bitcoin’s issuance transparency.
  • On-ledger visibility of escrow schedules, reinforcing our core value of transparency.
  • A trust-minimized environment, mimicking the atmosphere of Bitcoin while enjoying the speed, cost-efficiency, and scalability of the XRP Ledger.

Transparency remains our number one priority. By adopting these upcoming XRPL features, XRBitcoinCash will provide a verifiable, auditable framework that enhances trust, aligns with our mission, and ensures XRBC remains true to the spirit of Bitcoin while expanding the utility of the XRPL.
