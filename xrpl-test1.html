About XRBitcoinCash

Origin & Purpose

XRBitcoinCash (XRBC) launched in 2022 to demonstrate a simple, low‑fee, transparent asset on the XRP Ledger (XRPL). The project favors clear on‑ledger identity, machine‑readable docs, and safety‑first UX.

What We’re Building Now

  • Security Center: Health checks and verifiable, AI‑readable endpoints (health · price‑lite).
  • XRPL AMM & DEX: Live on‑ledger trading with trustline UX and a safety‑oriented trade page.
  • AI Collaboration (ChatGPT): Co‑authored docs and advisory audit language; no keys held, no signing.
  • Escrow Roadmap: Adopt time‑locked escrows for issued tokens when XRPL standardization allows.

How to Get XRBC

  1. Open a trustline in your Xaman (Xumm) wallet for XRBC.
  2. Use the on‑ledger DEX/AMM via our Trade page.
